Google is going nuclear

October 13, 2024

Google is exploring nuclear energy as a potential solution to power its growing data center needs. With the rising energy demands of AI applications, Google is considering integrating nuclear power, which could provide a stable and low-carbon energy source. This strategy aligns with the company's goals of ensuring energy sustainability while scaling up its data centers to meet future technological demands. The move follows similar initiatives from other tech giants like Oracle and AWS, who are also exploring nuclear options to meet energy requirements.

Apple Glowtime Event Triples Down on Apple Intelligence

September 12, 2024

At Apple's recent Glowtime Event, the company showcased its expanded focus on integrating AI across its devices, particularly through the Apple Intelligence platform. The event revealed updates across the Apple Watch, AirPods, and iPhone lineups, with Apple emphasizing on-device AI for tasks like health monitoring, personalized recommendations, and enhanced audio experiences. Notably, the AirPods Pro gained features that allow them to function as hearing aids, positioning Apple to make significant strides in the health tech sector​
